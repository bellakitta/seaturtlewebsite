Sea Turtles are in Danger!

Hi! I'm Bella! I'm a 2nd grader in MN. I made this web page because I love Sea Turtles and because I heard they are endangered.

Types of Sea Turtles

  • Green
  • Loggerhead
  • Kemp's Ridley
  • Olive Ridley
  • Hawksbill
  • Flatback
  • Leatherback

Ways We Can Help

  • Use the light of the moon(other lights can lure hatchlings away from the sea).
  • When boating be alert.
  • Do not litter!
  • Do not have campfires on the beach (sea turtles are known to be attracted to the light,crawl in and die.)
  • Tell people about how sea turtles are endangered.
